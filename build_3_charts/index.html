This is an pie chart of shootings by race. This tells the story of which races are being targeted by police. Many people already hold the belief that Black people are most targeted by police killings by the reality is that White people end up getting killed the most. What this graph does show is that Black people are being killed more than they are represented in the overall population which may be a sign they are being overly targeted.
